Work in progress log: At this point in the process of developing the present webpage, all of the basic elements seem to be present: it was designed to be a one-pager, so all of the necessary html is present. It was designed to have the foundations of user-responsiveness and so we see such javascript elements as (1) the automatic scroll feature, (2) the navbar mouseover feature, and (3) the navbar navigational highlighting feature. It was designed with the looping video back-drop in mind (a feature very much in vogue currently). Some crucial things are still missing, such as the contact form and the links to my other app-projects along with their case studies.
